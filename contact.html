Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me

What is your typical project process?

+

My process typically includes an initial consultation, project planning, design phase, development, testing, and launch. I maintain communication throughout to ensure the project meets your expectations.

How long does it take to complete a website?

+

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects with custom functionality could take 2-3 months. I'll provide a specific timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide website maintenance services?

+

Yes, I offer website maintenance packages to keep your site updated, secure, and performing well. These can be tailored to your specific needs, whether it's regular updates or as-needed support.

What is your payment structure?

+

I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. For larger projects, I can arrange milestone-based payments. I accept payment via bank transfer, PayPal, or credit card.
